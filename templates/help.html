Welcome to Help Page!

Here you can understand how to use Units Convertor properly.


How to convert units?

You can manually convert units by going to the units section(upper left corner, next to "Unit Convertor") and see the formulas.

Or you can acces advanced functions to do this.


How to access advanced functions?

First you should register (if you don't have an account).

You can do that by clicking on the register (upper right corner).

After that you need to complete the forms that appeared on your screen(if any problems the site will guide you with error messages).

If you have an account just click log in(upper right corner).

After that you need to complete the forms that appeared on your screen(if any problems the site will guide you with error messages).

Then the new functions should appear on the left side of the screen next to the types of conversion.

To log out you only need to click on log out(upper right corner).


How to select the type of conversion?.

You can look up and you will see the nav menu which has four types of conversion: weight, distance, time, temperature. Then you simply click on which one you want.


How to convert units?

After you selected what type of conversion do you want, a page will appear with the type of conversion you selected from the nav menu.

Then you should click on the box next to the 'From: ' label, and insert there the quantity you would like to convert.

Next you will click on the box next to the one you just completed and select the input unit type.

Now you will want to click on the box bellow those two and select the output unit type.
